ABOUT ME

The person behind the mods.

HUNTER @hunter6828
BeamNG.drive Mod Creator

Hey, I'm Hunter — I make skins, vehicles, and maps for BeamNG.drive. Everything here started on the official BeamNG.drive repository, but I'm moving my work to my own site so I have full control over how it's used and protected.

Right now that's 14 skin mods, one custom vehicle (the Gavril Heavystar II), and a map that's still a work in progress. Everything is free to download, as always.

All of my mods here are protected by a license/End User License Agreement (EULA) — check the License button on any mod card for the specifics.
